text
:character:r or l to indicate right or left:emotion:speech text dialouge blah blah blah:filename of sound to play if desired

do not break lines with blank space
like

this

--------------
example
:BF:r:Angry:My Balls Itch!:clownnoise

example, no sound
:BF:r:Angry:My Balls Itch!

character list (case sensitive):
BF
GF
Cuz

scenes
:SCENE:image name, usually a number:file of sound(leave blank if none):effect(fade,flash):colour(in hex):duration(decimal number):in or out(only used for fades)

example, no sound, white flash
:SCENE:1::flash:0xFFFFFFFF:0.5

example, sound, red flash
:SCENE:2:SNAP:flash:0xFFFF0000:0.5

example, no sound, black fade out
:SCENE:3::fade:0xFF000000:2.0

colour hexs are usually 6 numbers: 0xFF00FF

but FNF uses 8. the first two denote the alpha value: 0xFFFF00FF
